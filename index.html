Who We Are

A holding company
built for
the long term.

Canace LLC is organized as a diversified holding company, serving as the parent entity for a portfolio of operating businesses and advisory practices. Our structure enables each subsidiary to operate with focus and autonomy while benefiting from shared expertise, capital discipline, and governance.

Our principals bring experience across a broad range of domains — from leading multinational technology companies and building complex manufacturing operations, to advising senior government officials and executing high-stakes programs in national security contexts. That range of perspective is our structural advantage: we see opportunities others miss, and we have the operational and geopolitical fluency to act on them.

Our interests span consumer and enterprise technology, software development, aerospace and defense advisory, real estate development, and emerging markets — with particular depth in satellite communications, space infrastructure, and Latin America. Canace is privately held and built for the long term. We are not driven by short-term outcomes. Every business within our portfolio is built to last.
